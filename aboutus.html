About Us

I am pleased to introduce myself and the project I have been passionately developing. Inspired by my experiences and those of close loved ones, I created this initiative to amplify the voices of children affected by parental divorce. During separation, parents often become so engrossed in conflicts that they neglect their children’s emotional needs.

Parents may engage in behaviors aimed at discrediting the other, inadvertently brainwashing their children and causing profound emotional harm. Our project highlights these children’s diverse experiences and the severe, sometimes irreversible, damage to their relationships with the targeted parent. By sharing these stories, we aim to raise awareness and promote a more compassionate approach from parents or caregivers.

We seek to engage governments and affected individuals to advocate for policies that safeguard children’s emotional well-being, encouraging countries to adopt legislation that prioritizes children’s needs during parental conflicts. Additionally, we believe that providing support and education to parents, helping them heal their own emotional wounds, is transformative. By addressing past traumas, parents can create a healthier environment for their children, breaking the cycle of conflict and fostering positive family dynamics. Thank you for your time and support.
